Projects

Radius2D

Radius2D is a Physics Engine made for experiemntal purposes.
This currently have limited features which include,
Circle to Circle collision,
Circle to Line Collision,
And Spring Joints between Circles.

Galactic Battle

Galactic Battle is Topdown shooter game where you play as ship and have to fight against the fleet of enemy's ship.
When you destroy an enemy ship, you can collect their remains to grow your ship's size.
This was made for Kenney Jam 2022 with the theme of Growth.

Round Pong

Round Pong is game where play as Slider and you have to keep the ball inside the circle for as long as you can.
This game was made for the purpose of learning C# as this is my first C# project.

Seagull Survival

Seagull Survival is a Topdown Game, where you as a seagull have to dodge the poles on the sea surface.
This game is a submission to the Mini Jam 99, the theme was Birds.

Plankton Simulator

Plankton Simulator is a game where you have to survive as a Zooplankton, you have to eat phytoplanktons around you.
But be carefull, you are not at the top of the marine food chain.
This game is an entry for Github Game Off, where the theme was Bug.

Cream For Cone

Cream for Cone is platformer where you are a cone and you have to find the ice cream machine to fill you cone.
This is a submission to The Ice Cream Jam, as by the name the theme was Ice Cream.

Rotating Aim

Rotating Aim is platformer game where you as a ball have to reach to the star.
You will only jump in the direction of your arrow, which is constantly Rotating.
This Game was made for Kenney Jam 2021, with the theme of Rotation.

Bug Runner

Bug Runner is a platformer based endless runner game submitted for LowRezJam.
this have limited pixels as the limitation of the Jam was to make a game in canvas of 32 by 32 pixels or less.
This game was having multiple optional theme I chose two of them Mini Beast and Gravity is Your Enemy.
Fun Fact : This is my first time Game Jam participation.

Wrong Side Runner

Wrong Side Runner is my second public game.

Pong

Pong is my first publicly available game, it basically is a clone of The Classic Pong.
